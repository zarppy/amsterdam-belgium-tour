Arrival & Jordaan Walk

06:00

16:00
Jordaan Exploration

Architecture walk along Prinsengracht and Keizersgracht canals.

Museums & De Pijp

09:00
Art Morning

14:30

Wander the floating flower market and continue to Albert Cuyp Market.

Road Trip: Giethoorn

08:30
Rent Car

15:00
Zwolle Stop

Explore the medieval city walls of Zwolle.

Utrecht & Rotterdam

Friday, 2 October

09:00
Utrecht Dom Tower

Walk the unique double-level wharves in Utrecht.

13:00
Rotterdam Architecture

Visit Markthal and the Cube Houses.

05

Transition to Belgium

Saturday, 3 October

10:00
Train to Brussels

Return car in Rotterdam. Fast train to Brussels-Midi.

14:00
The Grand Place

Check-in central Brussels. Explore the Grand Place UNESCO square.

06

Brussels Icons

Sunday, 4 October

09:30
The Atomium

Photo stop at the Atomium structural icon.

14:00
Royal Palace

Walk the Royal Palace of Brussels facade and the Sablon district.

07

Brussels Deep Dive

Monday, 5 October

10:00
Art Nouveau

Architecture walk to see Horta's masterpieces. Evening in Flagey.

Meuse Valley Scenic Drive

Tuesday, 6 October

09:00
Namur & Dinant

Pick up Belgian rental car. Drive to Namur Citadel and Dinant.

14:30
Château de Walzin

Breathtaking cliffside viewpoint of the castle.

Bruges & Antwerp

Departure

Friday, 9 October

09:00
Last Chocolate Pass
