A Curated Journey Through

Netherlands & Belgium

29 September – 8 October 2026

PW-tour EU EDITION
01

Arrival & Old Amsterdam Walk

Tuesday, 29 September

06:00 - 09:00
Arrival at Schiphol

Land in Amsterdam. Take a direct taxi to your hotel in Jordaan. Settle in and leave bags.

11:00
The Core Loop

Walk to Centraal Station. Stroll down the Damrak to Dam Square for photos.

13:00
Snack Run

Grab famous Dutch fries at Fabel Friet. Walk to Van Stapele Koekmakerij for their legendary chocolate cookies.

02

Museums & De Pijp

Wednesday, 30 September

09:00
Cultural Morning

Start early at the Rijksmuseum or Van Gogh Museum.

14:00
Vondelpark & Market

A quick snap in Vondelpark. Visit the Albert Cuyp Market for local street food like stroopwafels.

03

Car Pick-up: Giethoorn

Thursday, 1 October

08:30
Car Rental

Pick up car Amsterdam. Drive 1.5 hrs north to the village of Giethoorn.

10:30
Boat Tour

Rent a 'whisper boat' to explore Giethoorn. Continue to Zwolle for a historic city walk. Overnight in Utrecht.

04

Rotterdam & Drive to Brussels

Friday, 2 October

10:00
Rotterdam Sprint

Visit the Cube Houses and Markthal. Grab lunch inside the market.

14:00
Into Belgium

Drive 2 hrs south to Brussels. Check into your hotel near the Grand Place.

05

Brussels Highlights

Saturday, 3 October

09:30
Atomium & Icons

Photo stop at the Atomium. Visit Mini-Europe if time permits.

14:00
Chocolate Tasting

Experience world-class chocolate at Neuhaus or Pierre Marcolini. Visit the Grand Place at night.

06

Scenic Meuse Valley

Sunday, 4 October

09:00
Namur & Dinant

Scenic drive to the Namur Citadel, followed by the iconic riverside views of Dinant.

14:30
The Belgian Countryside

Visit the viewpoint for Château de Walzin. Return to Brussels for the night.

07

Ghent & Bruges

Monday, 5 October

09:00
Ghent Waterfront

Stroll along the Graslei and see the Gravensteen Castle.

15:00
Fairytale Bruges

Continue to Bruges. Enjoy an evening canal boat ride or a stroll through the Markt. Stay in Bruges.

08

Bruges & Antwerp

Tuesday, 6 October

09:00
Bruges Morning

Final climb up the Belfry or visit to the Basilica of the Holy Blood.

13:00
Antwerp Architecture

Drive to Antwerp Central Station. Explore the Meir shopping district and the Cathedral. Stay in Antwerp.

09

Return to Amsterdam

Wednesday, 7 October

10:00
Drive Home

Leisurely drive from Antwerp back to Amsterdam (~2.5 hrs). Return car Amsterdam.

16:00
Final Night

Enjoy a final Dutch dinner and a quiet evening near Schiphol or in the city centre. Stay in Amsterdam.

10

Farewell Europe

Thursday, 8 October

07:00
Transfer to Schiphol

Early breakfast and taxi to Schiphol Airport for your morning flight to Melbourne.

Essential Travel Tips

Planning & Logistics

🏛️ Must-Visit Spots

  • Amsterdam: Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Book Early
  • Brussels: Grand Place, Atomium
  • Dinant: Collegiate Church riverside shot
  • Antwerp: Central Station architecture

🧇 Belgian Delights

  • Chocolate: Neuhaus (Galeries), Dominique Persoone (Bruges)
  • Waffles: Maison Dandoy (Brussels)
  • Fries: Fabel Friet (Amsterdam)

🚗 Logistics

  • Rental: Car picked up and returned at Amsterdam location.
  • LEZ: Register your car online for Brussels/Antwerp/Ghent zones!
