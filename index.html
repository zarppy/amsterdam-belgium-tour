A Curated Journey Through

Netherlands & Belgium

28 September – 8 October 2026

PW-tour EU EDITION
01

Arrival & Twilight Jordaan

Tuesday, 29 September

06:00 - 09:00
Arrive Schiphol (AMS)

Taxi to Linden Hotel (Jordaan). Traditional 3-star charm in the historic heart.

12:00 - 13:30
Jordaan Pit Stops

Grab lunch at Gifu Ramen Bar (Elandsgracht). Stock up on water at Albert Heijn (Westerstraat). For a classic snack, try the wall at Febo.

18:00 - 21:00
Twilight Silhouette Walk

Walk the 9 Streets. Stop by Pancakes Amsterdam (Prinsengracht) or enjoy the warm glow of the canals.

02

Museums & Canal Night Belt

Wednesday, 30 September

08:30 - 11:00
Rijksmuseum (Early Entry)

Beat the crowds. Focus on the Gallery of Honour and Rembrandt's Night Watch.

12:00 - 14:00
Museum Quarter & Park

Quick burger at Lombardo's (Nieuwe Spiegelstraat). Walk it off in Vondelpark. Afternoon matcha at Lera Cafe (De Pijp).

19:30 - 21:30
Dinner Pit Stop

Authentic margarita at nNea Pizza (Bilderdijkstraat) or local burrata at Cafe Marcella.

21:30 - 22:30
Canal Night Silhouette

Capture the illuminated 'Seven Bridges' view at Reguliersgracht.

03

Giethoorn Self-Navigation

Thursday, 1 October

08:00
Pick up Car (Schiphol/AMS)

Option A: 6-day rental (Return AMS 7 Oct). Easy and efficient for families.

15:00 - 16:30
Utrecht Pit Stop

After the drive, head to Firma Stroop (Lange Viestraat) for the ultimate fresh stroopwafel experience.

Stay: Eye Hotel Utrecht. 4-star architectural gem in a historic building.

04

Rotterdam & night-Grand Place

Friday, 2 October

09:00 - 12:00
Rotterdam Markthal

Architecture sprint. See the Cube Houses and the horseshoe market hall.

15:00 - 17:00
Drive to Brussels

Direct drive south (2 hrs). Check into Novotel City Centre or Adagio Grand Place.

20:00 - 21:30
Grand Place Night Illumination

Experience the medieval guildhalls glowing in gold. Spectacular night photography spot.

05

Brussels Hub (Fast-Paced)

Saturday, 3 October

06:30 - 08:30
Sunrise at Atomium

Empty square, soft light reflecting off the steel spheres. Pure architectural magic.

19:30 - 21:00
Mont des Arts Night Silhouette

Best night view of the city skyline and the Town Hall spire. A must-see before dinner.

06

Scenic Meuse (Castle Night)

Sunday, 4 October

09:00 - 13:00
Namur & Dinant

Drive to Namur Citadel. Continue to Dinant for the iconic river reflection shot.

20:00 - 21:00
Dinant Night Illumination

The Citadel and church reflected in the Meuse. Breathtaking at night.

Stay: Château de Namur. Countryside charm with estate views.

07

Ghent & night-Bruges

Monday, 5 October

08:00 - 12:00
Ghent Morning Walk

Walk the Graslei before the crowds. Gravensteen Castle entry at 10:00.

20:30 - 22:00
Bruges Night Reflections

Rozenhoedkaai (Rosary Quay) when the lights hit the water. Pure fairytale.

Stay: Grand Hotel Casselbergh Bruges. Period charm with modern comfort.

08

Dawn Bruges & Antwerp Depth

Tuesday, 6 October

05:45 - 07:30
Dawn at Rozenhoedkaai

Perfect blue hour reflection. Silent streets, mist on the canals. Best phototherapy session.

11:00 - 17:00
Antwerp City Deep Dive

Visit the MAS Museum or the Rubens House. Explore the fashion district.

19:00 - 21:00
Antwerp Night Skyline

Capture the Cathedral spire illuminated from the Linkeroever (Left Bank).

Stay: Hotel Franq Antwerp. 4-star boutique charm.

09

Antwerp Sprint & AMS Return

Wednesday, 7 October

09:00 - 12:00
Antwerp Railway Cathedral

Visit Antwerp Central Station. A quick architecture sprint before the drive.

14:00 - 17:00
Drive back to Amsterdam

Scenic 2.5 hr drive north. Return to the familiar canals of the Jordaan.

19:00 - 21:00
Car Return (Schiphol)

Return rental to Schiphol. Stay overnight in Hotel Mercier (Jordaan) for easy midday departure.

10

Farewell Europe

Thursday, 8 October

09:00
Transfer to Terminal

Quick breakfast. 10:00 check-in for 12:00 Midday Flight MEL.

Essential Travel Tips

🏛️ Must-Visit Spots

  • Brussels: Grand Place, Atomium
  • Ghent: Graslei Waterfront
  • Bruges: Rozenhoedkaai (Dawn)
  • Dinant: Riverside Reflection

🚗 Driving & LEZ

  • Zones: Register car online for Brussels/Antwerp!
  • Return: Always return car near Schiphol for morning flights.
